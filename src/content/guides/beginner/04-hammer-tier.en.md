# 🔨 Hammer & Tier Up Guide

## Hammer (Heroic gear) is the long-game core

> "Heroes change generations, but gear stays"

Mastery accumulation decides long-term gaps.
Even if it feels weak right now, investing in gear pays off long-term.

### 3 Hammer routines

1. Run **Bear Hunt** consistently every cycle
2. Concentrate gear enhancement on
   **frequently-used troop types** and **your main Arena heroes**
3. Gear **stays on your account** across hero generations
   — long-term investment

## Push your City Hall to advance Tier

### Top priority

City Hall level is the factor you'll feel **most strongly** later.
Troop tier upgrades are the key payoff.

### Speed-up priority (early)

| Rank | Target |
|------|------|
| 1st | **Construction** |
| 2nd | **Research** |
| 3rd | Troops / Healing |

### Core principle

> Don't spread speed-ups across many things.
> **Focus on City Hall and the prerequisite buildings.**

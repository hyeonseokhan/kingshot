# 🗺️ Beginner Growth Roadmap

A core growth guide for free-to-play and low-spending players.

## TL;DR

- Joining a top alliance early creates a snowball — chest, speed-up, and buff differences widen the gap fast
- VIP 7 and the Arena are the two pillars for low/no-spend accounts
- Knowing the priority of Hammer, gear, and shop purchases massively improves long-term efficiency

## ✅ Remember just this and you're halfway there

1. **Construction queue + March queue** — keep both running at all times
2. Get into a **top 1~3 alliance** as fast as possible
3. **VIP 7** is your first goal
   (daily routine: one Legendary universal shard)
4. The **Arena** is the supply route for Legendary gear and shards
5. **Hammer (Heroic gear)** is the long-game core
6. Knowing **shop efficiency** lets the same resources go much further

## ❌ Patterns that break beginners most often

| Mistake | Result |
|------|------|
| Lingering in any random alliance | Cumulative losses pile up |
| Spending a little on every event | End up with nothing useful |
| Spreading investment across heroes / gear / diamonds | Arena rank stagnates |
| Buying whatever the shop shows | Long-term efficiency suffers |

## 🔄 The full growth flow

The growth loop is **circular**:

> **Alliance** (chests / buffs) → **Arena** (Legendary gear) →
> **Hammer** (mastery) → **Higher Arena rank** → repeat

### 3-stage execution roadmap

| Stage | Goal |
|------|------|
| Stage 1 | City Hall construction, top alliance entry |
| Stage 2 | Reach VIP 7, climb the Arena rankings |
| Stage 3 | Lock in long-term competitiveness via Hammer and gear mastery |

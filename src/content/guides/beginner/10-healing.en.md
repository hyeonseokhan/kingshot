# 🏥 Healing Optimization Guide

Key information about **healing your troops during battles**.

## 🔹 Key risk

- During large-scale battles, many troops become **wounded**
- If your **infirmary is full**:
  - Wounded troops will **start to die**

> Therefore, always keep your infirmary **as empty as possible**.

## 🔹 Best healing method: Batch Healing

The most efficient way to heal quickly is **batch healing**.

### How to optimize healing

- Select troops with **short healing times** (e.g., ~45 minutes)
- This allows:
  - Faster healing cycles
  - More frequent **alliance Help** activations

### Alliance synergy

- The more players online pressing **Help**:
  - The faster your healing completes
- Short timers = **maximum Help efficiency**

## 🔁 Instant Heal Technique

Advanced technique to **maximize healing speed using alliance Help**.

### Core mechanic

- When healing a **small batch of troops**:
  - Alliance members can **fully reduce the healing time to zero**
  - Effectively **instant healing**

### Repeat strategy

- Heal a **small batch** (e.g., ~45 min worth)
- Let alliance Help **instantly complete it**
- Repeat the process

> This way you can heal **large numbers of troops in a very short time**.

### Optimization tip

- Test different batch sizes to find:
  - The **maximum number of troops**
  - That can still be **instantly completed** by current online members

### Key goal

Match your healing batch size with:

- The **number of active alliance members**
- Their **Help capacity**

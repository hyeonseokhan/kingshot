# 🏆 VIP & Arena Guide

VIP 7 and the Arena are the **two core growth pillars**
for free-to-play and low-spending accounts.

## Reach VIP 7 as fast as possible

### Why VIP 7?

Once VIP 7 is active, the **daily Legendary universal shard**
routine unlocks.

> This single routine alone widens
> long-term account growth significantly.

### The low-spend VIP path

1. **Claim VIP points** every day (consecutive)
2. Prioritize discounted **VIP Points** in the **Alliance Shop**
3. Prioritize discounted **VIP Points** at the **Wandering Merchant**

> Hit VIP 7 fast and you become
> **"the account that stacks shards every day"**.

## Why focus on the Arena

The Arena is the
**core growth hub** for low/no-spend accounts.

### 3 reasons the Arena matters

1. Higher rank means
   **steadier supply of Legendary gear and resources**
2. Gear earned in the Arena
   **fuels further rank climbing**
3. Long-term, it's the most stable growth route

### Growth loop

> Climb Arena rank → earn Legendary gear →
> reach higher rank → repeat

### Realistic targets

- Beginner goal: reach **top 100~500**
- Early on, prioritize **Archer / Cavalry gear**
- Use Hammer and gear enhancement to push Arena rank

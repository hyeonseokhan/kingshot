# ⚔️ Rally & Garrison Guide

Mechanics and hero usage when joining a Rally or a Garrison.

## 🔹 Hero Joining Skill — Only One Activates

When joining a rally or garrison, **only one hero skill is applied**.

### Key rule

- The activated skill comes from the hero in the **leftmost slot** (of three slots)
- Only the **top-right skill** of that hero activates (not the upper-left)
- All other heroes' skills are ignored

> Because of this rule, **left-slot hero choice** matters most when joining.

## 🤖 Auto Joining Setup

Configure your **Auto Join preset** properly to maximize your contribution to the alliance.

### Checkpoints

- Do **not include low-tier troops**
- Make sure your **heroes are correctly selected**

### Setup steps

1. Open the **War Window**
2. Tap **Auto Join**
3. Tap the **Gear Icon (Settings)**
4. Set your **first hero** as one of:
   - Chenko
   - Yeanwoo
   - Amane
5. Tap the **Equalize button** at the bottom
6. Manually set all troops **below Tier 6 to 0**
7. Tap **Save**

### Result

You're now ready to auto-join rallies efficiently and be a **valuable contributor** to your alliance.

## ⚔️ Joining Offensive Rally

Useful information for joining an offensive rally.

### Core strategy

Since only one skill activates:

- Always place your **strongest offensive hero in the left slot**
- This ensures the **best possible skill is applied**

### Recommended heroes

For offensive rallies and **Bear Trap**, use one of these in the left slot:

- Chenko
- Yeanwoo
- Amane
- Amadeus

### Optimization tip

- Upgrade these heroes' **skills as much as possible**
- Stronger skills lead to:
  - More powerful rallies
  - Higher **Bear Trap** scores

## 🛡️ Joining Defensive Garrison

Useful information for joining a defensive garrison.

### Core strategy

Same single-skill rule applies:

- Always place your **best defensive hero in the left slot**
- This ensures the **strongest defensive skill** activates

### Recommended heroes

For defensive garrisons, use one of:

- Howard
- Gordon
- Saul

### Optimization tip

- Upgrade these heroes' **skills as much as possible** to strengthen defense

### 🔄 Important transition

- After joining an **attacking rally** and successfully capturing an objective:
  - **Switch to defensive heroes immediately (ASAP)**
  - This ensures the captured structure is properly defended

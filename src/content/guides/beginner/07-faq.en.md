# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## Which alliance should a free-to-play join?

If possible, the **#1 alliance**, or at minimum a **top 2~3** alliance.

Differences in Alliance Gifts, Treasure Chests, Help speed, Rally frequency,
and Sanctuary / Ruins buffs cause **a huge growth gap**.

## Why is VIP 7 important?

At VIP 7, you unlock the routine of consistently securing
**one Legendary universal shard per day**.

It's the **long-term core** for free-to-play / low-spend accounts.

## Do I really need to play the Arena?

**Yes.** The Arena is the most important route for low/no-spend players
to steadily get Legendary heroic gear and core resources.

Hammer and gear enhancement are also directly tied
to Arena rank climbing.

## Why does Hammer (Heroic gear) matter?

Even when heroes change generations,
**gear and mastery stay**.

Critical for long-term account growth and combat-power retention.

## What should I buy first in shops?

Priority for free-to-play / low-spend:

1. Arena Shop → **Legendary chest**
2. VIP Shop → **Legendary universal shard + Hammer**
3. Wandering Merchant → **Resources**
4. Trial Shop → **Mithril**

> Buy **long-term resources that stay on your account**
> before what you need right now.

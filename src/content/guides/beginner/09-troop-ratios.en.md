# 🏹 Troop Ratios Guide

Guidelines for **optimal troop composition ratios** by situation.

## 🔹 Important principle

- These ratios are **ideal targets**
- However, in most cases:
  - It is **more important to use your highest-tier troops**
  - Raw strength often outweighs perfect ratios

## ⚔️ Recommended ratios (Infantry / Cavalry / Archer)

| Situation | Ratio |
|---|---|
| **Offensive Rallies** | 50 / 20 / 30 |
| **Defensive Garrisons** | 60 / 30 / 10 |
| **Bear Trap** | 10 / 10 / 80 |

### Optimization strategy

- Gradually work toward these ratios for each scenario
- Continuously upgrade troops to the **highest tier possible**

## 🧭 Mystic Trial — Ratios by Area

For **Mystic Trial** battles, area-specific ratios significantly improve progression speed.

Using the right composition helps you:

- Clear stages faster
- Improve efficiency
- Reduce failed attempts

### Recommended ratios per area (Infantry / Cavalry / Archer)

| Area | Ratio |
|---|---|
| **Coliseum** | 50 / 10 / 40 |
| **Forest of Life** | 50 / 15 / 35 |
| **Crystal Cave** | 60 / 20 / 20 |
| **Knowledge Nexus** | 50 / 20 / 30 |
| **Molten Fort** | 60 / 15 / 25 |
| **Radiant Spire** | 50 / 15 / 35 |

> Ratios are a starting point — adjust based on your hero / gear setup as you progress.

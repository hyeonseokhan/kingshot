# 🦸 Heroes & Skills (Beginner)

## Realistic low-spend goal

> **SR 4-star → first skill Lv.5 + troop tier upgrade**

## Core principles

### Hero training

- Don't blindly raise many heroes
- Compress resources into the **heroes you actually use often**
- Don't judge by raw combat power —
  judge by **whether they're used in Rallies and Arena**

### Skill investment

- For Rally heroes,
  the **first skill (Skill 1) efficiency** matters most
- Prioritize hitting Skill 1 Lv.5

### Resource allocation criteria

| Criterion | Invest? |
|------|-----------|
| Used often in Rallies | ✅ priority |
| Active in the Arena | ✅ priority |
| Just high combat power | ❌ later |
| Used only occasionally | ❌ later |

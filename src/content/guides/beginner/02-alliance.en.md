# 🏛️ Alliance Guide

> "You eat at least half the win right here"

Alliance choice is the single most important decision in Kingshot.
Aim for **rank 1**, or at minimum a **top 2~3** alliance.

## Core benefits of a top alliance

### Economic returns

- Daily accumulation of diamonds, speed-ups, and items via
  **Alliance Gifts + Treasure Chests**
- Faster **Help** speed (shorter construction / research times)
- More frequent **Rallies** (more rewards earned)

### Tech progression

- **Sanctuary / Ruins buffs** boost
  construction, training, and gathering efficiency

## ⚠️ The risk of mid-tier alliances

Staying too long in a vague mid-tier alliance:

- Politics, wars, and farm-target issues
  **shake your growth tempo significantly**
- The gap with top alliances widens steadily

## Required steps after joining an alliance

1. **Move your city fully inside** the alliance territory
2. Move **near the leader / core members** to join rallies easily

## 📋 Alliance system: just these basics

### 3 core features

| Feature | Description |
|------|------|
| **Donate** | Donate diamonds / coins |
| **Help** | Coin supply |
| **Rally participation** | Rewards / Hammer supply |

### Checklist before picking an alliance

> Make sure event times **match your active hours**.
> Mismatched timezones cause large long-term losses.

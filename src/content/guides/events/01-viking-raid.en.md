# 🛡️ Viking Raid Strategy Guide

Defend against the Viking invasion by killing as many enemies as possible
to **secure a high score**.

## 📅 Event overview

- **Cycle**: every 2 weeks (Tue / Thu)
- **Total rounds**: 20 waves
- **Success condition**: kill **at least 50%** of attacking troops
- **Failure condition**: 2 defeats

> **After your castle falls**
>
> - Personal defense is no longer possible
> - **You can still earn points by reinforcing other castles**

## 💯 Score structure

1. **Own-castle score**
   - When you defend Viking waves at your castle
2. **Reinforcement score (the high-score key)**
   - Earned when troops you sent as reinforcements get kills

> ⚠️ **Core principle**
>
> - **Reinforcement points are far higher** than keeping troops at your own castle
> - Keeping them at home is inefficient — empty out as much as possible

## 📊 Attack rounds

### Standard attacks

- **Rounds 1~6, 8~9, 11~13, 15~16, 18~19**
- Attack every alliance member's castle

### Online-only attacks

- **Rounds 7, 14, 17**
- Only attack **online players' castles**
- Offline players are not attacked

> Send reinforcements **prioritizing online players**

### Viking King's assault (HQ only)

- **Rounds 10, 20**
- Target is the **Alliance HQ**
- You must **march troops directly** to the HQ to defend

## ⚔️ Combat structure & troop usage

Combat goes in order: **Infantry → Cavalry → Archer**.

### Troop efficiency

- **New servers / low difficulty**: **100% Infantry** is highly efficient
- **Up to difficulty 11**: 100% Infantry covers everything
- **Late waves**: reaches Archer turn but score impact is minor

### Reinforcement composition

- Default ratio: **Infantry + Cavalry centric**
- Beginner / new server: **100% Infantry** recommended

## 🏠 Home defense strategy

### Why emptying your castle matters

> If **Infantry / Cavalry** stay at your castle,
> they fight Vikings and steal the kills.
> → Reduces the reinforcement score of allies who sent help!

### Defense management

- Keep only **Archers** at your home castle
- Send Infantry / Cavalry as **reinforcements to other alliance castles**

> ⚠️ **Healing warning**
>
> Healing during the event recalls troops, **losing reinforcement points**.
> Heal only after the event is fully over!

## 🏰 Alliance HQ defense

### Timing

- Recall troops only **after Round 9 / 19 fully ends**
  (pulling early loses reinforcement points)
- Send **one strong march** to the HQ after recall
- Once HQ defense is done, immediately go back to reinforcing allies

### Recommended ratio

| Round    | Recommended ratio          |
| -------- | -------------------------- |
| Round 10 | 100% Infantry              |
| Round 20 | **Infantry 5 : Cavalry 5** |

### 📌 PNX Alliance HQ defense protocol

> - **Default rule**: all 15 members participate in HQ rally
> - **Rally leader**: **Lv.30 with max rally capacity** (≥ ~900k)
> - **Per-person troops**: **60k each × 15 people** → safe total
> - Members eligible for **max command center** condition:
> - **Examples: Radu, Sseongbi, PirateKing, KassiNim**
>
> Adjusting troops for home defense varies by alliance style;
> **PNX runs flexibly with no forced rule.**

## 🦸 Hero usage

### When sending reinforcements

- **Goal is securing kills** → put offensive / rally-join heroes up front
- Recommended: **Chenko, Amadeus, Yeanwoo, Amane**
- Defensive specialists are inefficient here

### When keeping troops at home

- Keep only the 3 strongest defensive heroes; **send the rest as reinforcements / marches**
- Hero composition adapts flexibly to what you have

> Letting **reinforcements get the kills** beats getting kills at home for total score

## 🎬 Practical scenarios

### 🟢 Online player (full participation)

**1. 30 minutes before the event**

- Find **buddies (3~4 people)** and agree to mutual switching
- "I send Inf / Cav reinforcements to your castle → you send to mine"
- Troop ratio: **Infantry 8 : Cavalry 2** (beginner / new server: 100% Infantry)

**2. Right before event start**

- Empty your castle: send all Infantry / Cavalry as **reinforcements to your buddy**
- Keep only **Archers** at home

**3. During Rounds 1~9**

- Stay online and **maintain reinforcements** to online players' castles
- **Round 7 (online-only)**: confirm all 4 buddies are mutually reinforced
- Even one defeat is fine — reinforcements continue (no point loss)

**4. Round 10 (HQ defense)**

- Recall troops only **after Round 9 fully ends**
- Immediately send **one strong march** to HQ
- Keep Archers at home
- After HQ defense → **go back to reinforcing**

**5. During Rounds 11~19**

- Same flow as Rounds 1~9
- **Rounds 14, 17 (online-only)**: re-check buddy online status

**6. Round 20 (final HQ defense)**

- Recall troops **after Round 19 fully ends**
- Send to HQ at **Infantry 5 : Cavalry 5** ratio
- Keep reinforcements at home castles

**7. After event end**

- Once all troops return, **start healing** (no healing during event!)

### 🔴 Semi / offline player

- **Empty your castle (mandatory)** — send reinforcements to buddy then log off
- Rounds 7, 14, 17 don't attack offline players' castles
- If possible, log on to **participate in HQ defense only** (Rounds 10, 20)

## 🎯 Summary & high-score strategy

1. Send all troops as reinforcements to other castles —
   **empty your own castle**
2. If you have many troops, pull **Infantry → Cavalry first** and
   **leave only Archers**
3. Reinforcement ratio: **Infantry 8 : Cavalry 2**
   (beginner / new server: **100% Infantry**)
4. Send reinforcements
   to **online players' castles** as much as possible
5. Strong specs → use the
   **1:1 direct switching strategy**
6. **No healing during the event** (loses reinforcement points)

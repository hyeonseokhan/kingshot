# 🏰 Castle War Strategy Guide

Castle War is a large-scale capture event between alliances.
The first alliance to reach the cumulative occupation time wins.

## 📋 Basic rules & preparation

### Time & win condition

- KST **9 PM ~ 3 AM**
- Win when cumulative occupation time is reached

> 📌 **PNX Alliance:** win condition is **2 hours 30 minutes** of cumulative occupation

### Positioning (relocation)

- Starting **1 hour before**, you can relocate into the red zone
- Move as close to the castle as possible

> 📌 **PNX Alliance:** relocation direction is decided on the day (TBD)

### Team assignment

- You are pre-assigned to **Team 1, 2, or 3** —
  remember your team and act accordingly

## ⚔️ Castle capture & defense strategy

### Initial attack (solo)

Start with solo attacks using your **strongest Tier 9~10 hero**,
not Rally attacks.

> 📌 **PNX Alliance:** troop ratio **Infantry 5 : Cavalry 2 : Archer 3**

### Switch to defense

- The instant the castle is captured, switch defensive heroes and brace

> 📌 **PNX Alliance:** offensive / defensive heroes vary by situation

### Double rally (when solo attacks fail)

If another alliance steals the castle and your attack fails:

- **Team 1 and Team 2** run double rallies
  with 1~2 second offsets to chain hits

> 📌 **PNX Alliance:** keep the **5:2:3** ratio even on double rally

## 🗼 Tower capture & rear support

### Tower capture

- **Team 2 and Team 3** capture nearby towers
- Same method as the castle (solo attack → switch defensive heroes)

### Wounded healing (alliance coordination)

- Members not in combat should
  **actively help heal the wounded**
- Get alliance help fast and
  process healing in **40-minute chunks**

### Troop reinforcements

- Reinforcement troops also keep the **5:2:3** ratio
- But **Infantry get wounded most**, so
  keep replenishing Infantry

## ⚠️ Most important caution

> During the war, players' cities are
> packed extremely tight in a small zone.
> **Never accidentally attack another player's city** —
> stay extra careful!

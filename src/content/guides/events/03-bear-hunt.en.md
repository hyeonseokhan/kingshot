# 🐻 Bear Hunt Strategy Guide

Bear Hunt is a battle where troops don't die and the fight lasts a fixed
**10 turns**, with the final score based on **cumulative damage**.

## 💡 Core: 90% of damage comes from your Hero

Adding troops matters far less than
**how strong a skill you can land**.

Damage influence ranking:

1. **Exclusive weapon**
2. **Hero skill**
3. **Hero star tier**

> **90% of Bear Hunt damage** comes from the hero.
> Focus on hero specs over troop count!

## 🦸 Manual placement of key heroes is mandatory

**Do not** use the system's "strongest hero" auto-placement.

### How to place

1. **Cancel** the existing placement
2. Manually send your key hero to the **leftmost slot**

### Recommended key heroes

- **Chenko**
- **Yeanwoo**
- **Amane**
- **Amadeus**

> When marching together, only **Skill 1** of joining users applies —
> prioritize heroes with higher skill level or star tier.

## 🏹 Troop deployment strategy

Many players think more troops mean more damage, but
**troops have the smallest impact on damage**.

### Deployment style

- All troops in one big march ❌
- **Even split** or **proportional split** across marches ✅

### Troop ratio

- **80% Archer** recommended

## 🎯 Summary

To raise Bear Hunt damage:

1. Prioritize **hero exclusive weapon and skills**
2. **Manually place key heroes** (Chenko, Yeanwoo, etc.) leftmost first
3. Split troops **evenly across marches**, Archer-centric
